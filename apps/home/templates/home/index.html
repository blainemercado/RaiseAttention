Our Mission to RaiseAttention

We believe there is nothing more valuable today than a person's attention. Just look at the Ad, Sports and Entertainment industries for the monetary value, but beyond that we desire the attention of the people closest to us as a reflection of their care, and when we can focus the attention of large groups of people on a single cause amazing things can be accomplished.

As mentioned, the Ad industry puts a monetary value on your attention, and we are using the ads on this site to fund one great cause every month. Thanks to your attention we can raise money and help make a real impact. Please help us further RaiseAttention, and money, by sharing with your friends and family.
